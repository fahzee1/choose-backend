How do I become a volunteer?

Below you can find our contact info. Please reach out to Kelechi Ogbuehi our Founder and CEO.

Where does my donation go?

All funds raised for Team Destiny Foundation are used for the sole purpose of building homes in Nigeria. We have a professional team and board of directors who oversee all activities.

Can I get photos and videos of homes being built?

Yes! We'll be updating our website with more photos and videos of the current projects underway. It's exciting to see your hard earned money helping those less fortunate.

Can I join the team?

Of course! Get in contact with us!

Be your brother's keeper

If you would like to help us build homes for families living in poor conditions like the one pictured, then help us and donate. We can do it if we work together
